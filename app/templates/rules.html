Senior Assasins Official Rule Book


Official Rules

What is Senior Assasins?

Senior Assassins is a game played by high school seniors where participants attempt to "eliminate" each other from the game through various means. The game can be an exciting way to build camaraderie and add some excitement to the final days of senior year, but it is important that all participants understand and follow the rules to ensure the safety and fair play of all involved.


Eligibility

Only high school seniors are eligible to participate in Senior Assassins. All participants must sign a consent form agreeing to abide by the rules of the game.


Gameplay

Each participant is assigned a target to "eliminate" by shooting them with a water gun or by placing a sticker on them. Once a participant eliminates their target, they inherit their target's target and the game continues until there is only one player left.


Safety

Participants should not engage in any behavior that could result in physical harm or damage to property. Participants should also not engage in any behavior that could be perceived as threatening or intimidating.


Restricted areas

Certain areas may be designated as off-limits, such as school grounds or private property. Participants should not enter these areas to pursue their target.


Time restrictions

The game may be limited to certain hours of the day or certain days of the week. Participants should not engage in gameplay outside of the designated times.


Disqualification

Participants who break the rules may be disqualified from the game.
